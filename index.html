The Material Of The Future

Carbon nanotubes are one of the most innovative materials ever invented and stand ready to disrupt everything from sailboat racing to skyscraper construction, aerospace, automotive, and far beyond.

  • 100x stronger (pound for pound) than steel
  • High chemical resistance
  • Super light-weight
  • Readily workable
  • Incredibly flexible
  • Conductive or EM shielding
  • From 1 mm up to 2 cm long

Imagine a plane twice the size and half the weight of a 747, a city floating on the ocean, or an inflatable, modular space habitat system. Carbon nanotubes will enable each of these and more.
