Long Carbon Nanotubes For Composites

At 30 times stronger than kevlar, our long carbon nanotubes are dispersable, workable, and ready for mixing into epoxies and plastics (as seen in the image above).

Short carbon nanotubes (<100 microns) pull out of expoxies and other materials. Long (1 mm to 2 cm), strong carbon nanotubes form a complete, intertwined mesh of support and can not pull out. Their strength is transferred to the matrix making it stiffer and stronger as well as electrically and thermally conductive.

The Material Of The Future

Carbon nanotubes are one of the most innovative materials ever invented and stand ready to disrupt everything from tennis racquets to spacecraft, sailboat racing, aerospace, automotive, and far beyond.

  • 30x stronger than kevlar, 20x stronger than carbon fiber
  • High chemical resistance
  • Light-weight

  • From 1 mm up to 2 cm long
