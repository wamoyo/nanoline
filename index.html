The Longest Carbon Nanotubes On The Market

At 2 cm and longer, we are making the longest carbon nanotubes on the market. Long carbon nanotubes are safer, confer greater strength in composite materials, and have more applications than short carbon nanotubes.

Ten Fold Increases To Strength And Beyond

A 1,000% increase in strength is possible with just a 0.5% portion of carbon nanotubes.

The Material Of The Future

Carbon nanotubes are one of the most innovative materials ever invented and stand ready to disrupt everything from sailboat racing to skyscrapers construction, aerospace and automotive, and far beyond.

  • 100x stronger than steel
  • Super light-weight
  • High chemical resistance
  • Readily workable
  • Conductive or shielding
  • Incredibly flexible

Imagine a plane twice the size and half the weight of a 747, a city floating on the ocean, an inflatable, modular space habitat system. Carbon nanotubes will enable each of these and more.
